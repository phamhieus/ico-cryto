What is SpaceShipNFT token?

SSNFT is one of the crypto NFT platforms, and a breakthrough in the field of NFT in particular and cryptocurrencies in general.

What is NFT?

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. Where "fungible" is often used in economics, it means that the individual units of an asset are interchangeable and essentially indistinguishable from one another.

What make SSNFT token different from other NFT token?

The difference of SSNFT lies in the projects it is aimed at, such as farming, staking and mining.

What is SSNFT business model?

SSNFT is a token used in virtual reality games, we can exchange them for in-game items and vice versa. Some of the virtual reality games that SSNFT will start are: Puppet Fever, Play With An Adorable Virtual Pet, Curious Tale Of The Stolen Pets, ..
